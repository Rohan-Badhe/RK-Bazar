About SPICE BAZAAR

Your trusted supermarket delivering quality products at affordable prices.

Our Story

SPICE BAZAAR is one of India’s leading supermarket chains operated by Avenue Supermarts Limited. Founded in 2002, SPICE BAZAAR focuses on offering customers high-quality products at the best possible prices. With stores across multiple states, SPICE BAZAAR continues to grow while maintaining its commitment to value and service.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of products including groceries, fresh fruits and vegetables, personal care items, home essentials, apparel, packaged foods, and much more — all under one roof.
